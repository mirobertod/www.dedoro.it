What makes me, me
A brief summary about who I am

Born in 1994 in Milan, since early age I noticed computer science would have been my passion. I think I was lucky because my father had already bought his first computer and then I started very soon to fiddle around.

After graduation I started working in that field. My first job role was system administrator. Throughout my career I realised how much impact computers can have on the world.

Nowadays I’ve over five years experience and I’m hunger to learn new stuff everyday. That right there is what makes me to build this website.

Fascinated by cloud computing and open source philosophy, I hope technology will help human beings to get a better world.
